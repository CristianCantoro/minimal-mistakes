• WikiLinkGraphs' Snapshots dataset:
    • This dataset contains snapshots of Wikipedia articles (namespace 0) taken yearly on March, 1st for the languages de, en, es, fr, it, nl, pl, ru, sv. The snapshots were taken on March 1st, for the years between 2001 and 2018 (included). The dataset has been produced by processing Wikimedia's history dumps. See the complete list of fields [+].
