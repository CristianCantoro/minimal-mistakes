### Is this dataset used in currently in-review or future papers that cite it? This dataset is published as part of a paper *"WikiLinkGraphs: A complete, longitudinal and multi-language dataset of the Wikipedia link networks"*, currently under review at [ICWSM 2019](https://www.icwsm.org/2019/index.php). This dataset is published as part of the ENGINEROOM project and it will be cited in the related scientific publications.
